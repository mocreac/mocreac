Agimut Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What data is stored

Agimut stores annotation comments, CSS selectors, page URLs, and per-site enable/disable preferences. This data is used solely to display your annotations on the correct pages.

Where it's stored

All data is stored entirely on your device. Annotations live in your browser's localStorage (per-site), and host preferences are kept in chrome.storage.local. Nothing is ever transmitted to any server.

What we don't collect

Agimut collects no personal information, no browsing history, no analytics, no telemetry, and sets no cookies. The extension makes zero network requests.

Third parties

None. No data is shared with any third party. There are no accounts, logins, or external services.

Deleting your data

To delete all Agimut data, uninstall the extension or clear your browser's local storage for the sites where you used it. Host preferences can be removed by clearing chrome.storage.local via the browser's developer tools.
